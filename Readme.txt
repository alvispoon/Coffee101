Coffee101 - The coffee tutorial app for android
This is a simple app to show basic coffee brewing method. It can teach everyone to make a coffee at home.

What's new
1. 5 basic brewing method introduce
	a. Pour Over
	b. French Press
	c. Expresso
	d. Syphon
	e. Ice Drip

2. Include all required equipment and simple step by step guideline.

How to Install
1. If you use android studio, you can just import the project
	There are two key activities - MainActivity.java and Brewing1.java
	a. MainActivity.java is the menu page with 5 coffee brewing icon
	b. Brewing1.java is the detail brewing page with equipments and step by step guidance
